What We Do

Planning vacations sucks. There is so much time and effort spent wasted on things that just simply get in the way of the fun. Vacation planner is your one stop shop for planning out that radical family vacation that you've been dreaming about all year.

Planning

You can now create a vaction and share it with family or friends that will be with you. From there, all users can suggest places to travel, fun activities you can do while traveling. You can suggest dates and figure out a time that will be good for everyone. Recently added is a comment section where users can message each other so you can discuss all aspects of your trip.
